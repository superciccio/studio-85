LEPANTO GOLD SIDEBOARD

Characterised by impressive formal elegance and contemporary charm, this sideboard bears witness to innovative design with traditional materials. The brass inlay for each sideboard door stands out for its bold and diagonal cut. The intersections of materials elegantly interrupt the regular form, defined by the union between the grain of the marble and the inlaid brass to create an apparently flowing design. The doors are fashioned of Rosso Lepanto marble, and the top is in Portoro Marble. All the internal and external finish in amazing brass, making it a dynamic addition in a minimalist or post-modern decor.

Materials: Lepanto Marble, Brass
